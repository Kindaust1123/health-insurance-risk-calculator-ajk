Health Insurance Risk Calculator 2020

Kindustries, Inc.

Instructions

This application helps you determine the risk associated with insuring a new customer. The application will ask you for the customer's body-mass index, blood pressure, age, and family history of disease. After answering all of the questions, the application will give you a final risk score as well as the customer's final risk category. You are able to calculate and evaluate the risk scores of customers as many times as you would like.
